Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Starling Calculator does not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell personal information or app usage data from users.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data, contact information, location information, usage analytics, identifiers, diagnostics, or any other data from the app.

On-Device Processing

Calculations, voice input handling, and related app functions are intended to operate on the user’s device. We do not operate a backend service for collecting or retaining user data through the app.

Third-Party Services

Starling Calculator is not designed to send user data to the developer or to third parties for analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s data practices change in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before the new practices take effect.

Contact

For privacy questions about Starling Calculator, please use the developer contact or support information provided on the App Store product page.
